Key Principles

🎯
Treat the underlying psychiatric condition first wherever possible
🧬
Individualise by comorbidities, age, and type of sleep problem
⚠️
Avoid antihistamines in elderly — anticholinergic burden
💊
DORAs are effective but often more expensive than alternatives
🚫
Avoid BZDs and BZRAs in patients with substance use disorders
🌗
Middle-of-night awakenings: zaleplon or zolpidem SL are specific options
